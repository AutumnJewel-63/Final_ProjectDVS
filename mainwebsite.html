Code Differently provides hands on training and education through coding classes that gives participants the technical and cognitive skills they need to excel in technology-driven workplaces.

Our Programs

1000 Kids Coding

The Code Differently 1000 Kids Coding program was created to expose New Castle County students to computing and programming. The 1000 Kids Coding courses are designed for all experience levels, no experience required.

Return Ready

The Return Ready Workforce Training Initiative is funded by New Castle County CARES Grant to assist residents of New Castle County with returning to work with technology skills needed for today’s technology-driven workforce.


Pipeline DevShops

Pipeline DevShop is a youth work-based learning program. Youth participants experience working in a real software development environment while sharpening their technology and soft skills.

Platform Programs

Platform programs are designed for high school graduates, college students, career changers, or professionals looking to develop the technology job readiness skills for today’s workforce.

Why Code Differently?

75% of our high school participants will continue their education or career in technology related fields.

95% of our college participants received offers for internships or full-time employment in technology organizations.

96% of our program participants identify as ethnically underrepresented.

41% of our participants identify as women.

Our Approach

We designed each program on the foundation of five strategic pillars:

Attraction:

We employ innovative initiatives to spark participants' curiosity about the tech industry.

Exposure:

We expose participants to the amazing world of technology and coding.

Engagement:

We keep participants engaged by fostering more in-depth learning of principles and concepts.

Ownership:

We help participants develop a sense of ownership of their new skill set by providing opportunities for them to develop real technology solutions.

Empowerment:

We empower participants to inspire others to explore tech careers.

Strengthening the technology ecosystem by creating mutually beneficial relationships between communities and corporate partners.
